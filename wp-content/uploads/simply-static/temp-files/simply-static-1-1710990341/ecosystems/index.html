Business Ecosystems

The web transforms many business ecosystems. Find out how the Web Consortium meets particular industry needs, and how everyone benefits.

Which business ecosystems are particularly impacted by the web?

The Web Consortium and its members, with help from the public and the web community, focus on business ecosystems that the web transforms.

  • E-commerce

    Making payments and commerce easier and more secure on the web.

    Learn more about our work in the E-commerce ecosystem

  • Media & Entertainment

    Turning the web into a professional platform for delivery of media content, and immersive experiences.

    Learn more about our work in the Media & Entertainment ecosystem

  • Publishing

    The web is the premier publishing platform.

    Learn more about our work in the Publishing ecosystem

  • Network & Communications

    Shaping the future of telecommunication on the web.

    Learn more about our work in the Network & Communications ecosystem

  • Data and knowledge

    Linked Open Data is a key part of the digital transformation which positively impacts society.

    Learn more about our work in Data and knowledge

  • Web of Things

    Bridging disparate technology stacks to allow devices to work together and achieve scale, thus enabling the potential of the Internet of Things by eliminating fragmentation and fostering interoperability.

    Learn more about our work in the Web of Things ecosystem

  • Automotive & Transportation

    The Open Web Platform provides a rich ecosystem for vehicles and transportation.

    Learn more about our work in the Automotive & Transportation ecosystem

  • Web Advertising

    Making online advertising more effective and privacy-preserving.

    Learn more about our work in the Web Advertising ecosystem
